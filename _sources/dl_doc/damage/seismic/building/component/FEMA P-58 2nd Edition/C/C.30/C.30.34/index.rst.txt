

**************************************
C.30.34 - Independent Pendant Lighting
**************************************

The following models are available:


.. raw:: html

   <p class="dl_comp_name"><b>C.30.34.001</b> | Independent Pendant Lighting - non seismic</p> 
   <div>

| Horizontal light fixtures 4 to 16 ft long suspended 12 to 24 inches from ceiling.  No seismic design or bracing.  Hung from ceiling above by two or more rods or cables.  
| Construction Quality: Normal
| Seismic Installation Conditions: All
| Notes: Low cost industrial hung lighting in warehouse or gymnasium that is not part of suspended ceiling or hanging from a suspended ceiling space.


Suggested Block Size: 1 EA

   <p class="dl_comp_name"><b>C.30.34.002</b> | Independent Pendant Lighting - seismically rated</p> 
   <div>

| Horizontal light fixtures 4 to 16 ft long suspended 12 to 24 inches from ceiling.  Hung from ceiling above by two or more rods or cables.  Fixture and rods or cables are either designed for seismic movements or are laterally braced to prevent sway.
| Construction Quality: Normal
| Seismic Installation Conditions: All
| Notes: Low cost industrial hung lighting in warehouse or gymnasium that is not part of suspended ceiling or hanging from a suspended ceiling space.
